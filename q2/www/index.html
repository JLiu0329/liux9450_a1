Welcome to Web Design University

Web Design University is where all you dreams can come true. WDU is devoted to excellence in teaching, learning, and research, and to developing leaders in many disciplines who make a difference globally. The University, which is based in Waterloo, Ontario has an enrollment of over 20,000 degree candidates, including undergraduate, graduate, and professional students. WDU has more than 360,000 alumni around the world.

Computer Science Department

Engineering and applied sciences at WDU have a long and distinguished history, beginning with the creation of the Super Fake School of 2018. During the 19th and 20th centuries, the structure to support faculty and research in engineering applied sciences underwent several reorganizations (ranging from a graduate school, department (several), and division). WDU's Computer Sciences department is now world-renown.
